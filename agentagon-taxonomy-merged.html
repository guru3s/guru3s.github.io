The model is easiest to remember as a cycle: evidence triggers investigation, the origin gets a failure label, the cause explains the layer to change, and the fix becomes a verified artifact that improves future detection.

Signals Observable clues that start and guide an investigation.

Investigation Funnel

Use this sequence in the product. It turns a noisy trace into a defensible recommendation without mixing symptoms, causes, impact, and fixes into one label.

1. SignalsMany cheap clues from spans, evals, users, changes, cohorts, systems, and evidence-health checks.
2. OriginThe earliest causally relevant node or entity where the run moved onto a bad path.
3. Failure ModeThe precise "what failed" label attached to that origin, not merely the final symptom.
4. Root CauseThe evidence-backed explanation of why the failure happened and which layer should change.
5. ImpactCustomer visibility, correctness risk, recurrence, cost, fixability, and confidence.
6. FixContainment, durable artifact, owner, verification, guardrail metric, and rollback path.
7. LearnCreate or update a detector, eval, replay, monitor, memory, runbook, or taxonomy example.

Boundary Rules

The merged taxonomy stays intuitive because every label has one job. If a label answers more than one question, split it or move it to the right layer.

Impact Is A Separate Lens

Impact should not be encoded into the failure label. A rare safety leak and a common shallow answer both need prioritization, but they should be scored separately from taxonomy classification.

Customer Visibility

Hidden, degraded, visible, blocking, user-escalated, or account-level.

Correctness Risk

Wrong, incomplete, unsafe, unsupported, noncompliant, or merely inefficient.

Recurrence

Single trace, cluster, cohort spike, trend, release regression, or drift.

Cost And Tokens

Latency, token burn, cache misses, tool spend, retries, and infra cost.

Fixability

Prompt, config, data, code, workflow, model, or product-level effort.

Confidence

Evidence quality, localization certainty, evaluator agreement, and review status.

What Was Merged

The original proposal supplied the deep taxonomy and competitor mapping. The synthesis supplied stricter layer boundaries, a better investigation funnel, expanded observability coverage, and cleaner product presentation.

Signals versus findings, drift, historical backfills, fix artifacts, measured lift, demotion, and regression monitoring.

FutureAGI

OpenSRE

Incident RCA discipline, evidence provenance, log compaction, infra/runtime causes, causal chains, validated claims, and remediation steps.

Patronus AI

Evaluator families, custom taxonomy definitions, insight cards, prompt fixes, safety scoring, and trace-level evidence fields.
